About D & A Convenience Store


What once started as a dream to bring quality everyday goods and
essentials to neighboring residents soon turned into what is now the
D & A Convenience Store.


What others see as a simple supply and demand chain in Saekyung,
Marigondon, we see as a business opportunity — a chance to provide
easy access and a wide selection of consumer goods. D & A
Convenience Store is co-owned by Angie Martinez.


Proudly built from the ground up, our store continues to grow and
adapt to meet the daily needs of our customers. Over time, we’ve
expanded our offerings to include more essentials that cater to both
long-term residents and visitors on a quick weekend getaway. At D &
A Convenience Store, we’ve got you covered!


Our goal is to provide a pleasant and enjoyable shopping experience
for all our valued customers. We also offer delivery services across
Saekyung Village 1 — bringing your essentials straight from our store
to your doorstep. Because here at D & A Convenience Store, your
convenience is our top priority
